  FinTrack — Personal Finance Tracker
  Standalone Windows Executable
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HOW TO RUN:
  Double-click FinTrack.exe. No installation needed.

FIRST RUN:
  1. Sign up with your email and password.
  2. Verify your email (check spam if not visible).
  3. Sign in and start tracking!

CURRENCY:
  Settings → Currency to switch between PKR, USD, EUR, GBP,
  INR, AED, SAR, CAD. Live rates are fetched automatically.
